Challenges Girls Face in STEM

Despite the progress, girls still face several challenges in STEM, such as:

  1. Stereotypes – The misconception that STEM is a "male-dominated" field discourages many girls from pursuing it.
  2. Lack of Role Models – Many young girls don’t see enough women in STEM leadership roles to inspire them.
  3. Limited Access to Resources – In some areas, girls have fewer educational opportunities in science and technology.
