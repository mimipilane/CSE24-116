Instuitions offering courses in STEM

The instuitions with courses related to STEM as follows;

  • Botswana Accountancy College (BAC): Computer Systems Engineering: This course combines computer science with engineering principles, focusing on designing and optimizing systems, hardware, and software for efficient operations in various sectors. Business Intelligence and Data Analytics: This program covers techniques for analyzing business data and extracting valuable insights, preparing students to make data-driven decisions and improve business processes.
  • Botswana International University of Science & Technology (BIUST): BEng Mechanical and Energy Engineering: This program integrates mechanical engineering principles with energy systems, preparing students to address energy challenges through innovative engineering solutions. BSc Computer Science: This course offers foundational knowledge in computer science, covering areas such as programming, algorithms, and data structures, aiming to produce graduates capable of developing technological solutions.
  • University of Botswana (UB): Engineering: Programs in various engineering disciplines, such as Civil, Mechanical, and Electrical Engineering. Science: Courses in fields like Biological Sciences, Physical Sciences, and Mathematics. Health Sciences: Programs related to medical and health-related fields.
  • Botho University: Information Technology: Courses focusing on software development, networking, and IT management. Business Computing: Programs that combine business principles with IT solutions. Health Informatics: Courses that integrate healthcare with information technology.
  • Limkokwing University of Creative Technology: Bachelor of Science (Hons) in Information Technology: Focuses on the use of computers to store, retrieve, and send information. UN WOMEN Bachelor of Arts (Hons) in Creative Multimedia: Combines creativity with multimedia technology. Bachelor of Arts (Hons) in Games Design: Centers on designing and developing interactive games. Bachelor of Arts (Hons) in Digital Film & Television: Covers aspects of digital filmmaking and television production.
